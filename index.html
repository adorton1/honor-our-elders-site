About the Gathering

Honor Our Elders is an annual community event hosted by the Intertribal Community Council of Texas (ICCT). This gathering recognizes the wisdom, leadership, and lived experiences of Native American elders who have carried our stories, traditions, and strength across generations. The event includes a shared meal, gifts, cultural activities, and time for connection.

Support Our Elders

ICCT aims to support 100 Native American elders this year through meals, gifts, and community recognition.  Your contribution directly funds the food baskets, gift cards, and support items provided to each elder.  Every donation helps us honor those who have carried our traditions forward.

Where Your Donation Goes

Your contribution directly supports American Indian elders in the Dallas–Fort Worth community. Donations help provide shared meals, gift cards, food baskets, and essential items that are distributed during the Honor Our Elders gathering. Each gift ensures that elders feel valued, supported, and honored for the wisdom and leadership they have shared across generations.

ICCT is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization, and every tax-deductible donation goes toward community programming, cultural events, and elder support services. Thank you for helping us uplift and care for the elders who continue to guide our community.
